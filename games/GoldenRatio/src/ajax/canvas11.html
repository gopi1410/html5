The Golden Ratio

Since the 20th century, many artists and architects have developed their works in this proportion - believing this ratio to denote BEAUTY and BALANCE in their work.



In mathematics, two quantities are in golden ratio if the ratio of the sum of thw quantities to the larger quantity is equal to the ratio of the larger to the smaller.



It is denoted by the symbol φ (phi).
