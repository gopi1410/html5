The Vitruvian Man



The Vitruvian Man was painted by Leonardo Da Vinci - inspired by the work of ancient Roman architect Vitruvius, who described human body as a system of proportions.

It has been proven that the painting of The Vitruvian Man, by Leonardo Da Vinci shows the symmetry of the human body and was made to study the proportions of the male human body.
